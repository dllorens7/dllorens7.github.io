Day 10   (12 / 11 / 24)

Today we worked on the batteries all class. This consisted of spot welding 22 batteries together to get an end result of 4 volts and 77,000 Mah of power, which theoretically is enough to keep the Cyberdeck running for 24 hours continuously. The first picture shows the current state of the cyberdeck, with a digital multimeter next to it. In the welding process, we connected the positive leads together and then the negative leads together. The clamps were used to hold the batteries together while we super-glued all the cells together. The last picture is of us wrapping the batteries in electrical tape to protect the electrical contacts.
